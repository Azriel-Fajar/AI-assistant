## What This Skill Does

Generates Instagram captions and hashtag sets for Rielcode's account (@rielcode). Content is professional, direct, and targeted at small businesses looking for a website.

**Brand context:**
- Business: Rielcode -- website development
- Services: custom websites, budget landing pages, simple e-commerce
- Target audience: small businesses, local entrepreneurs, startups
- Tone: professional and direct. No emojis. No filler phrases.
- Instagram: @rielcode

## Steps

### 1. Gather content details

Ask the user:

- What type of post is this?
  1. Portfolio showcase (showing a completed project)
  2. Service promo (promoting a specific service)
  3. Educational / tip (sharing a useful insight about websites/business)
  4. Engagement / question (asking the audience something)
  5. Other (describe it)

- Any specific details to include? (e.g. project name, service name, stat, specific message)

Ask both questions in one message. If the user already provided context when invoking the skill, skip what's already clear.

---

### 2. Generate 3 caption options

Write 3 distinct caption options. Each caption must:
- Be under 150 words
- Open with a strong first line (the hook -- this is what shows before "more")
- Be direct and professional
- End with a clear call to action (e.g. "DM us to get started.", "Link in bio.", "Comment below.")
- Have no emojis
- Have no em dashes

Label them Option 1, Option 2, Option 3.

Make each option distinct in angle or tone:
- Option 1: benefit-focused (what the client gains)
- Option 2: credibility/proof-focused (results, process, or expertise)
- Option 3: direct/offer-focused (clear pitch or invitation)

---

### 3. Generate hashtags

Provide one hashtag set of 10-15 tags. Mix:
- Niche-specific: #WebDevelopment #WebDesign #LandingPage #Ecommerce
- Local/regional (Indonesia-based): #JasaWebsite #JasaPembuatanWebsite #WebDesignIndonesia
- Business audience: #SmallBusiness #UMKM #BisnisOnline
- Brand: #Rielcode

Do not use overly generic tags (#business, #success, #entrepreneur) unless they are in the niche mix.

---

### 4. Offer a variation

After presenting the 3 options, ask:
"Want me to adjust the tone, shorten any of these, or generate a version in Bahasa Indonesia?"

---

## Rules

- No emojis anywhere in the captions.
- No em dashes.
- No overly salesy language -- keep it confident, not pushy.
- Always include a call to action at the end of each caption.
- If the user asks for Bahasa Indonesia, rewrite (do not translate word-for-word) -- keep the same directness.
- Hashtags go below the caption, separated by a blank line.
