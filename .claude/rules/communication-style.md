# Communication Style

## Format
- Use bullet points or tables -- avoid large paragraphs
- Keep responses short and to the point
- No preamble or filler phrases

## Tone
- Internal (working with Azriel): Direct and professional
- External (client messages, proposals, Instagram): Professional and direct

## Rules
- No emojis
- No em dashes
- No overly formal language
- One idea per sentence where possible
